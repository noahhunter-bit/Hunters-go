Partner With HuntersGo

We believe businesses and ministries can work together to bring real, lasting transformation to communities in South Sudan and beyond. Sponsorship Tiers

Choose a monthly investment that aligns with your business or organization's heart for impact. Every level makes real ministry possible.

Harvest Partner — $50/mo

Village Builder — $150/mo

Covers local ministry worker stipends, translation work, and pastoral support.

Field Sustainer — $300/mo

Funds transportation, food distribution, and on-the-ground outreach for an entire week.

Mission Catalyst — $500–$1000/mo

Underwrites training events, village outreaches, and major ministry initiatives.
