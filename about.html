About Us

Our Story

We’re Noah and Hanna Hunter — a husband and wife called by God to bring His love to the nations. We met during college, both pursuing degrees that would help us serve in cross-cultural ministry. Our shared passion for missions quickly became the center of our life together.

Our Calling

Through prayer, mentorship, and hands-on mission work, we have felt a clear and consistent call to the mission field — specifically to regions in Africa, including South Sudan. God has placed a burden on our hearts to make disciples, teach, and equip local believers to reach their own communities.

Our Vision

We believe the Great Commission isn’t a distant command but a present invitation — to go, serve, and love as Jesus did. Our long-term goal is to plant ourselves where the Gospel is least known, working alongside local churches and organizations to see spiritual and practical transformation take root.

Life & Preparation

As college students, we balance ministry preparation, education, and marriage with joy and humility. We’re learning, growing, and trusting God for every next step — whether that’s a classroom, a fundraising season, or a field far from home.
