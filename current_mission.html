Heading Back to Las Vegas — 2026

We’re preparing to return to Las Vegas with Trinity Bible College’s GO Trips program to join Operation for Freedom in outreach, prevention, and community care. Below you’ll find our story, our partners, and how to walk with us in prayer and support.

The Mission

In 2026, we’ll be returning to Las Vegas with a team from Trinity Bible College through the GO Trips program. There, we’ll work alongside Operation for Freedom — a faith-based outreach dedicated to ending trafficking and exploitation by meeting people with compassion, practical help, and the hope of Jesus.

Operation for Freedom’s work centers around direct outreach, prevention, and care partnerships. In Las Vegas, that means mobilizing teams into the community, engaging those vulnerable to exploitation, and building trusted relationships that open doors to long-term restoration. It’s kingdom work that meets people where they are and offers a way forward.

GO Trips at Trinity Bible College are immersive service experiences that give students hands-on ministry training in cross-cultural contexts. For us, this means joining God’s heart for people by stepping out in obedience and compassion—serving alongside those already faithfully present in their city.

Every prayer, every word of encouragement, every act of generosity brings us one step closer to reaching those who need to hear that they are seen, valued, and loved.
