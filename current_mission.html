Our Story & Calling for Las Vegas

We’re returning to Las Vegas in 2026 as part of a GO Trips team from Trinity Bible College to partner closely with Operation for Freedom. Operation for Freedom focuses on reaching those trapped in exploitation through prevention outreach, direct engagement, and partnerships with local agencies and volunteers. Their work centers on empowering vulnerable populations, building relationships that lead to trust and connection, and creating practical pathways — whether that looks like outreach in hospitality districts, street ministries, or connecting people to care and resources. We’re joining this work because it brings gospel-centered compassion to people in dangerous situations, and because we believe presence, relationship, and steady love open doors that programs alone cannot. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

About Operation for Freedom
Operation for Freedom exists to end sex trafficking and commercial sexual exploitation through local outreach, prevention events and direct care. In Las Vegas they run outreach teams, collaborate with partners, and provide resources and connections to those in need — combining practical help with restorative support. We will be serving alongside local staff and volunteers in public outreach, resourcing vulnerable people, and supporting community awareness efforts. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

About GO Trips (Trinity)

GO Trips (Global Opportunity Trips) are Trinity Bible College’s cross-cultural, faculty-led spring missions experiences. They integrate classroom learning with practical ministry and provide students a supervised environment to serve, learn and grow in cross-cultural ministry contexts. GO Trips are a key part of Trinity’s spring semester formation and often partner with ministries like Operation for Freedom to place teams where needs and learning opportunities intersect. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
