The Bread of Life

Have you ever been on a road trip and forgotten your lunch? That gnawing hunger in your stomach, the way everything else becomes a blur because your body needs something to sustain it… That’s what life feels like without Christ, the true bread that satisfies our souls.

"I am the bread of life. Whoever comes to me will never go hungry, and whoever believes in me will never be thirsty." – John 6:35

Jesus is inviting us to a deeper nourishment than anything this world can offer. Just as our bodies crave physical bread, our hearts crave Him. When we seek Him daily, reading His Word, praying, and resting in His presence, we are fed in ways nothing else can satisfy.

Reflection Question 1

In what areas of your life have you tried to fill yourself with things other than Jesus? How has that worked out?

Reflection Question 2

How can you make more space in your daily rhythm to “come to the bread” and let Jesus truly satisfy your soul?

Next Step

Choose one daily practice this week to feed your soul on Christ: it could be reading a chapter of the Gospel of John each morning, journaling about how Jesus has been your sustainer, or sharing this passage with a friend and reflecting together.
